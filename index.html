Hello yogi!

Welcome to Hanna's home, where I, Hanna, teach you yoga at a level accessible to your skill level. I teach small classes (no more than 5) to assure that each yogi gets the space and attention they need to gain the most out of the class. Whether you are an experienced yogi or a complete beginner does not matter, we will adjust the asana's for each and every body.

So if you are looking to try yoga for the first time, or find your new yogi community book your first class at Hanna's Home.

About Hanna

Hanna has been traditionally schooled in Hatha and Ashtanga Yoga in Rishikesh India, and then later Iyengar and Gosh. Her friendly and hands on approach focuses on making unfamiliar movements more familiar, which makes her an excellent teacher for beginners and those looking to better their alignment.
